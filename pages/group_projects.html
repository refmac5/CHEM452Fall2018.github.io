Special Project and Grading Policy


PRESENTATION

You will be required to give a 15-20 minutes PowerPoint presentation to explain your project. Presentations must be clear, well edited and presentable. The presentation will be shown in class and each group member must present and be prepared for the questions and answer period following their presentation. Assignment of presentation dates will be on a voluntary basis or by lottery.
