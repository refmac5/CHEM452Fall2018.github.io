Assignments:

1. Post the possible translated protein sequence (First amino acid of expressed protein is usually Methionine)

2. Pick the protein sequence starting with “MKDCSNGCSAECTGEGGSKEVV", , locate the sequence of “TPPLDG” and design primers (forward primer and reverse primer) to mutate the underlined T to D.

More practice: design primers to perform the following mutations

1. "SPVGPY", mutate S to D

2. "TPATILK", mutate T to D
